How Super’s computer-use cache works

1) Observe real computer actions

Super operates a real browser/desktop to complete multi-step tasks like authentication, navigation, and form filling.

2) Save a reusable action trace

The system stores stable steps and decision points as a cache, not brittle selectors.

3) Replay + adapt on repeats

On the next run, Super reuses the cache and adapts only what changed, reducing cost and errors.

4) Human-in-the-loop safeguards

For sensitive actions, Super pauses for confirmation instead of auto-purchasing.
